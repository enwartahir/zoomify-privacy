Zoomify Documentation

Complete guide to installing, configuring, and using Zoomify.

Overview

Zoomify connects your Shopify store to your Zoom meetings, allowing you to display products to meeting participants in real time. Participants can browse products and click Buy Now to purchase directly through your Shopify store — without ever leaving Zoom.

Requirements

  • Active Shopify store
  • Zoom account (any paid plan)
  • Zoom desktop client version 5.7.3 or later

Installation

Step 1 — Install from Shopify

Install Zoomify from the Shopify App Store. Open your Shopify admin and navigate to Apps → Zoomify. The app will request permissions to read your products and orders — click Install.

Step 2 — Select Products

From the Zoomify dashboard click Products in the navigation. Browse your store products and click Add to Zoom on any product you want to feature during meetings. You can select multiple products. Selected products are shown with a green highlight.

Step 3 — Connect Your Zoom Account

From the Zoomify dashboard click Connect Zoom. You will be redirected to Zoom's authorization page. Review the permissions and click Allow. You will be returned to the dashboard with your Zoom account connected.

Step 4 — Add Zoomify to Your Zoom Client

Install Zoomify on your Zoom account by visiting the Zoom Marketplace listing and clicking Add. This only needs to be done once.

Step 5 — Use During a Meeting

Start a Zoom meeting as the host. Click Apps in the meeting toolbar. Find and open Zoomify. Your selected products will load automatically. The app will send an invitation to all participants so they can view and interact with the products independently.

How Participants See Products

When the host opens Zoomify, all meeting participants automatically receive an in-meeting notification inviting them to open the app. Participants click Open and the product panel appears in their Zoom client. Participants do not need to install anything.

Each participant can independently browse the products and click Buy Now on any product. This opens the Shopify product page in their browser where they complete the purchase through your normal Shopify checkout.

Managing Products

You can change your selected products at any time from the Zoomify dashboard in your Shopify admin. Changes take effect immediately — the next time you open Zoomify in a meeting, the updated product selection will be shown.

Removing the App

To uninstall Zoomify go to your Shopify admin → Apps → Zoomify → Delete app. All data including your product selections, Zoom connection, and meeting history will be permanently deleted.
