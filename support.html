We are here to help you get the most out of Zoomify.

Getting Started

  • Install Zoomify from the Shopify App Store and open it from your Shopify admin
  • Select products you want to feature during your Zoom meetings from the Products page
  • Connect your Zoom account by clicking Connect Zoom on the dashboard
  • Start a Zoom meeting and open Zoomify from the Apps panel in your Zoom client
  • The app will automatically invite participants to view your products

Frequently Asked Questions

Do participants need to install anything?
No. Participants receive an in-meeting invitation when the host opens Zoomify. They click Open and the product panel loads automatically.

Where do purchases happen?
When a participant clicks Buy Now, they are taken to your Shopify product page in their browser to complete the purchase. All payments are handled by Shopify.

Why are my products not showing in the meeting?
Make sure you have selected products from the Products page in your Shopify admin and that your Zoom account is connected on the dashboard.

Why is Zoomify stuck on Initializing?
Make sure your Zoom desktop app is up to date. Zoomify requires Zoom desktop client version 5.7.3 or later.

How do I disconnect my Zoom account?
You can reconnect your Zoom account from the Zoomify dashboard in your Shopify admin at any time.

How do I uninstall Zoomify?
Go to your Shopify admin → Apps → find Zoomify → click Delete. All your data will be removed automatically.
